PCR Step by Step

Pour a chemical onto the wipe, then wipe the counter
1
Clean the countertop with 70% ethanol, 10% bleach, then water — in this exact order. Wipe the counter with a clean wipe between each chemical.
2
In a sterile tube, use your pipette to add reagents in this order: Water → Buffer → dNTPs → Forward & Reverse Primers → Taq DNA Polymerase.
Vortex the buffer before adding it.
3
Once you have the master mix, vortex it.
4
Set parameters on your PCR machine:
Denaturation: 30s @ 94–95°C Annealing: 30s @ 50–65°C (depends on primer Tm) Extension: 1 min @ 72°C Cycles: repeat 25–35x
5
Put your mixture in the PCR machine, close it, start it, and wait.
