Pipette box holders

laboratory organizers designed to keep serological pipettes, canisters, and micropipette tip boxes safely stored and easily accessible

Pipette box holders are racks, stands, or carousels designed to organize and store pipette tip boxes on the lab bench. They keep multiple tip boxes (often in different sizes—standard, filtered, or extended-length) upright, accessible, and neatly arranged to save workspace. Common types include rotating carousels for easy one-handed access, stackable modular racks for compact storage, and wall-mounted or magnetic holders for space-saving. They don't affect the tips themselves but help prevent clutter and reduce the risk of knocking over boxes during work.
